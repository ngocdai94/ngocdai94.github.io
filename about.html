Ubiquiti Network

What is Ubiquiti Network?

Ubiquiti Network is an Enterprise Networking company, which is specialized on making Internet Network components for Enterprise such as Gateway, Switch, and Access Points. Since these products are specialized as an Enterprise, they have been tested so that they can be operated 24/7.

Enterprise networking products are usually very expensive. However, Ubiquiti Network has been going out of their way to make their networking products afforable. Compare to other Enterprise Networking companies such as Cisco, Meraki, and Aruba, Ubiquiti networking products are cheaper than them.

Ubiquiti Network has been growing so fast that now they now have more products focusing on security such as Surveillance System and consumer product to reach more customer.

What is Ubiquiti Surveillance System?

Ubiquiti Surveillance System is a Network Video Recording (NVR) system that involves Unifi Power Over Ethernet (PoE) Switches to power PoE Cameras, a Linux Server, and storage where video recording can store on it. There are two Ubiquiti Surveillance systems, which are Unifi Protect and Unifi Video.

Unifi Protect, which is running on Unifi Cloud Key Gen2 Plus, is designed for small houses and businesses. It’s very compact. However, there’s a limit on how many devices that can be put on the Unifi Protect

In the other hand, Unifi Video is more flexible than Unifi Protect. It’s a hardware based device on a Linux server. In short, the server can handle a lot of Unifi Cameras as long as the server is strong enough.

What is Amazon Web Services?

Amazon is not only well known for their E-Commerce Online shopping but also well known for providing a lot of infrastructure services for business. For example, AWS provides online computing is known EC2, which a small business can use their virtual machine to run software. More AWS services will be discussed on the bullet listed below.

What are the benefits of using Amazon AWS?

Amazon is not only the biggest Online Retailer but also, it is the biggest Cloud Services in the world. Amazon data center is expanding, and it is improving and offering more services. When using Amazon as your services, you will have the following benefits.

  1. Extremely fast uploading/downloading to the AWS services anywhere in the world.
  2. Affordable price. Pay as you go and only pay to the services that you are using.
  3. Strong and up-to-date security.
  4. Back-end enterprise networking products worry-free.
  5. AWS is a scalable service. No matter how big your businesses are going to be, AWS will be always scalable to your businesses’ size.
